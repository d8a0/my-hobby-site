The Boss


Frankie is the big boss. We work for him and carry out his orders to a high standard, always going above and beyond.

Frankie is a rescue axolotl who came to us in poor health but recovered marvelously and now has got us wrapped around his little finger (he does have little fingers).

His favourite meal consists of worms and his preferred pastime activity is to wiggle around the tank with the grace of a sausage.
